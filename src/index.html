      Sync scroll

      Browsers block cross-origin scroll access. To make sync-scroll work, add this one-liner to the <head> of your staging site (drop it into the Kobe starter theme once and it works on every client). The app sends and receives scroll events via postMessage.

      <script>window.addEventListener('scroll',()=>parent.postMessage({rt:'scroll',y:scrollY,x:scrollX},'*'),{passive:true});window.addEventListener('message',e=>{if(e.data?.rt==='scroll')window.scrollTo(e.data.x,e.data.y)});</script>
